LED Status Quick Reference

Green — Hydrated You're in great shape. Keep it up.
Yellow — Replenish Drink water or electrolytes soon.
Red — Rehydrate Drink water or electrolytes now.
Blue (flashing) — Low Battery Remove cartridge and charge.
White (flashing) — Error Contact support@intake.health
Red Dot — Battery Warning Plan to charge soon.
Green Dot — Fully Charged Ready to reinstall.
