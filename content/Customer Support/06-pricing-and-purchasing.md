## Pricing Overview

InFlow is available as a direct purchase or through the lease program. Most customers — especially teams and organizations that want a hassle-free experience — find the lease program to be the better long-term value.

---

**What's included in the lease:**

For larger configurations or custom swap schedules, contact support@intake.health for a quote.

---

## Purchase vs. Lease: Which Is Right for You?

### Choose Purchase If:
- You have staff capacity to manage periodic charging and humidity maintenance
- You want a one-time cost with no ongoing fees
- You need only 1–3 units (small setup)
- You prefer to own your equipment outright

### Choose Lease If:
- You want zero maintenance burden — no charging, no desiccant kits
- You want the assurance of always having fresh, fully functional units
- You are deploying 4+ units and managing a team or facility
- You want a predictable annual cost
- Your staff is already stretched and doesn't have bandwidth for device management

## Purchasing Process

### Direct Orders

InFlow can be purchased directly from Intake Health. Contact support@intake.health or visit www.intake.health to place an order. Once an order is confirmed, units ship immediately from in-stock inventory.

### Distributor / Reseller Orders

InFlow is available through select distributors and resellers. If your organization requires procurement through a third-party vendor (as is common in industrial, healthcare, and government contexts), Intake Health can provide a formal written quote addressed to your organization that your procurement department can use to initiate a purchase order through your preferred vendor.

To request a formal quote, contact support@intake.health with:
- Your name and organization name
- Billing/shipping address (or purchasing contact if going through a third party)
- Quantity needed
- Whether you're interested in purchase or lease

### International Orders

Contact support@intake.health to discuss international shipping options and pricing.

## Common Purchasing Scenarios

**A pro sports team wants 16 units for their locker room:**
- Recommended: Lease at $5,220/year for 8 active units + 8 spares on rotation, or purchase 4 four-packs with master carton pricing.
- Best fit: Lease, since athletic training staff rarely has time to manage maintenance.

**An oil rig wants to cover 8 urinals across two floors:**
- Recommended: 2 × 4-packs ($5,198) or lease at $5,220/year.
- Consider purchasing an extra 4-pack as backup if running 24-hour operations.
- For 10+ assets (multi-site rollout), volume pricing applies.

**A university athletic department wants to outfit multiple sports:**
- Start with 1–2 4-packs for the primary sports, then expand.
- Lease program available. Contact for multi-team pricing.

**A reseller wants to stock InFlow for athletics customers:**
- Contact support@intake.health for reseller and distribution partnership inquiries.
