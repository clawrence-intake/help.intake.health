### LED State Quick Guide
| Light | Meaning | Action |
|---|---|---|
| 🟢 Green | Well hydrated | You're good — keep it up |
| 🟡 Yellow | Mildly dehydrated | Drink water or electrolytes now |
| 🔴 Red | Significantly dehydrated | Rehydrate immediately |
| 🔵 Blue (flashing) | Battery low — needs charge | Remove cartridge and charge |
| ⚪ White (flashing) | Error | Contact support@intake.health |
| Red Dot | Battery low warning | Plan to charge soon |
| Green Dot | Battery fully charged | Ready to reinstall |
